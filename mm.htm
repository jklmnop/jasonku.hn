Objectives

  • To score points by capturing your opponent's army.
  • To have the highest cumulative score for eaten M&Ms
  • To have the highest cumulative score for uneaten M&Ms
  • To have the highest average of points, eaten and uneaten

Set-up

Supplies: M&Ms, Scorecard, Cups*, Napkins* *optional

  1. Start with a new bag of M&Ms
  2. Open bag and place army into ranks or any configuration you find suitable
  3. Tally your pieces for a grand total and for amount of each hue
  4. You are now ready to play!

Rules

Primary: Red, Yellow, Blue
Secondary: Orange, Green
Neutral: Brown
    Begining a game/round:
  • The begining of each game is based on the grand total of pieces for each player
  • The begining of each round is based on the total amount of points for each player
  • The player with the least amount of pieces/points gets the advantage to make the decision to take the first offensive move
  • Should there be a draw, both players will select the side of a coin, and the decision will be based on the flip
    Attacking:
  • A primary may capture a primary of the same hue
  • A secondary may capture a secondary of the same hue or the combination of primaries that create its hue
  • A neutral may capture a neutral of the same hue, a secondary and remaining primary, or all three primaries.
    • ex: brown => green (yellow)
    Capturing/Prisoners of War:
  • After a successful attack, the offensive player will eat the attacking piece/pieces and capture the defendant's piece/pieces to be used in the next round
